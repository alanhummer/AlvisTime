    Welcome to Alvis Time! Time card entry integrated with Jira. We believe Alvis Time is simple and easy to learn and use. 3 simple steps:

    1. Set week you want to enter time for (scroll buttons at the top)
    2. Enter time into Jira ticket buckets for each day of the week (expand/collapse the issue groups)
    3. When "done", submit your time for approval (button at the bottom, turns green when you hit required # hours)

      4. BONUS: You can now see "Remaining Estimate (REM)" for your issues get updated as you enter time. You can even adjust it yourself in Alvis Time.
    PROJECT WORK: For LANDS END, we are trying to maximize time on capital projects. Our big rocks. 3 simple points:

    1. Make sure to book any meeting, planning, onboarding, etc. time as it relates to projects, to that project. Alvis Time has project generic project buckets for this stuff.
    2. Goal is 90% of time to project work. Alvis time has totals, percentages and "red"/"green" indicators to highlight this
    3. If there are any issues that do not have project/sub-project designation, we'll need to add it to them so that we can account correctly. Feel free to do that with the Jira links from Alvis Time.
    KTLO SUPPORT WORK: For LANDS END, we are trying to keep all support as "Keep The Lights On" (Mandatory Support). 3 simple "primary" support buckets:

    1. Problem Incidents - break/fix, severity issues, hot fixes of production systems. Does not include low priority QA defects that didn't make a release.
    2. Operations - Non-discretionary preventative maintenance. DB tuning, environment/stacks maintenance.
    3. Audits and Compliance - participation in SOX and PCI audits (evidence gathering etc), or engineering work in response to an audit issue.
    OTHER SUPPORT WORK: For LANDS END, we are trying to keep all other "Support" to a minimum (Descretionary Support). 3 simple "secondary" support buckets:

    1. Security - Security approved engineering changes to achieve and/or remain compliant with IT Security Policy. S/B vetted by IT Security.
    2. Tech Debt - Addressing "Debt" of previous engineering trade-offs made to hit cost and budget constraints. Assess against short term revenue generating, cost reduction, risk reduction. Try to move Tech Debt items into LE.com enhancements
    3. Requests/Other - (do not use) - Small, yet high priority business requests under 80 hr. Note: this existed before "Enhancements".
    That's it. Have fun with Alvis Time!
